Everything about NCC

National Cadet Corps at ABIT

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a tri-service organization, comprising the Army, Navy, and Air Force wings, engaged in grooming the youth of the country into disciplined and patriotic citizens.

At Aryabhatt Institute of Technology, our unit is dedicated to developing character, comradeship, discipline, and the ideals of selfless service among our students.
